Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · Applies to orbiter.sh

Short version

orbiter.sh uses no tracking cookies and no analytics. This page exists to be transparent about the minimal technical cookies that may be set by the hosting infrastructure.

What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file stored in your browser by a website you visit. Cookies can serve different purposes: some are technically necessary for a site to function, others are used for analytics or advertising.

Cookies we use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
__cf_bm Necessary Cloudflare bot management — set only if the hosting provider uses Cloudflare. Does not track you personally. 30 min
_cfuvid Necessary Cloudflare rate limiting. Not used for tracking or advertising. Session

Depending on your hosting region, none of the above cookies may be set at all. We do not set any first-party cookies ourselves.

What we do NOT use

  • No Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, or any Google tracking
  • No Meta Pixel or social media tracking
  • No advertising or retargeting cookies
  • No session tracking or user profiling
  • No A/B testing frameworks

Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. The following links explain how to do this in the most common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Note that disabling technically necessary cookies may affect the basic functioning of this website.
